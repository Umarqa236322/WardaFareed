About Me

Helping teams ship stable, user-ready software with confidence

I'm a Top-Rated QA Engineer with 4+ years of experience and a track record of 100+ projects delivered, a 5-Star client rating, and a 100% Job Success Score on Upwork. I help SaaS, AI, Web, and Mobile teams catch the issues that matter before real users ever see them — so your product earns trust from day one.

My focus is on identifying hidden bugs, edge cases, and performance risks early in the development cycle. I specialize in testing AI and chatbot-driven applications — including LLM flows, conversational accuracy, and voice AI — alongside full-stack manual testing across web, mobile (iOS & Android), and SaaS platforms. I bring hands-on expertise in functional, regression, exploratory, API, payment gateway, accessibility (WCAG), and performance testing using JMeter.

My process is structured and outcome-driven: requirement analysis, test planning, execution across real devices (iPhone, Android, BrowserStack, MacBook, Windows), clear bug reporting with steps, severity, and visual proof, and thorough retesting for bug-free releases. I've supported products across AI & chatbot platforms, SaaS, FinTech, Healthcare, E-commerce, EdTech, and Travel — collaborating closely with product and engineering teams in Agile environments.

If you need a QA partner who can catch critical issues early and help ship a reliable, user-ready product, I'd love to help make that happen.
